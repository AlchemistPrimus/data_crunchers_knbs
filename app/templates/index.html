About us

Datacrunchers are the Africa statistics hackathon winners that aimed at promoting good data use for KNBS. Given dataset we were able to innovatively make something good out of it, that is visualizations that can be used to study distribution of resources in different counties in our country.

This can help our country in appropriate distribution of resources to tackle SDG, providing solutions through data collected by the government.
